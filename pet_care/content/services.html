We offer a multitude of services for all of your pets' needs:

  • Grooming: Whether your pet needs a bath or nails trimmed we can do it here!
  • Training: Does your pet feel the need to "go" wherever he/she pleases? Do you want them to great you with a paw when you want them to "shake?" Well, give us some time with your pet and we shall get them to behave or do amazing things!
  • Daycare: Need to leave your pet with someone have nobody to watch your furry family member? Leave them in our care and we will be sure that your pet will great you with a grin :). Daycare rates start at $35/day.
  • Vacation Boarding Services: Are you leaving town for a vacation? Keep your pet out of a kennel by leaving them with us while you are out enjoying the sun! The rates for Boarding are typically lower than daycare but you have to leave your pet with us for at least four days.
